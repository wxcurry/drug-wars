# Gameplay Design

The core loop is short and repeatable:

1. Inspect current city prices.
2. Buy underpriced goods.
3. Travel to another city.
4. Sell into spikes, shortages, or jackpot buyers.
5. Manage heat, gang pressure, health, inventory capacity, and cash.

## Economy

Each city rolls a finite market from item rarity, regional affinity, city price index, difficulty, day, seed, and current event. Not every item appears in every city.

Market event types:

- shortage: selected item spikes
- surplus: selected item drops
- market crash: risky low entry point
- legendary buyer: rare high-value sell opportunity

Trend arrows communicate price motion against the previous market.

## Risk

Risk rises when the player lingers, travels through dangerous territory, trades aggressively, or gambles. Police and gang events use current heat, gang pressure, city law, city gang influence, difficulty, and character escape bonuses.

Outcomes include:

- checkpoint dodge
- police sweep
- arrest scare
- fines and bail
- inventory confiscation
- jail time skip
- gang ambush
- robbery
- health damage

## Progression

The player grows by:

- improving cash
- increasing reputation through trades
- upgrading inventory capacity
- exploiting rare markets
- learning each city's supply profile

Harder difficulties increase starting debt, danger scaling, and price pressure.
